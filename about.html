Hi, I'm Giovanni — a Senior RPA & Intelligent Automation Engineer based in Brussels, building hyperautomation for high-compliance financial infrastructure at Euroclear. Over the past 4+ years I've designed and shipped 15+ production automations that process 20,000+ items a month at sub-2% exception rates — cutting manual handling by 40–60% and giving back 3,000+ staff hours a year.

My toolkit spans UiPath, Intelligent Document Processing, OCR and the Microsoft Power Platform, and lately I've been pushing hard into AI-augmented workflows and low-code tooling. Since April 2026 I also wear a Business Analyst hat — bridging business stakeholders and engineering teams from requirements all the way through delivery.

Before automation I was a deep-learning developer and co-authored two peer-reviewed MDPI papers on machine learning. I love turning messy, manual processes into clean, auditable systems — and in my spare time that same itch becomes the little web apps you'll find back in AppVault.
